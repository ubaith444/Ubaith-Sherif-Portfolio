# Design System

## Design Direction

The visual direction is premium, minimal, editorial, and technical. The site should feel like a serious AI Engineer portfolio rather than a product dashboard or generic personal site.

## Palette

- Background: `#fafaf7`
- Foreground: `#0b0f19`
- Light surface: `#ffffff`
- Muted text: `#475569`
- Accent blue: `#2563eb`
- Dark background: `#09090b`
- Dark surface: `#18181b`
- Subtle purple is used only as a secondary atmospheric accent.

## Typography

The stack uses Geist, Inter, SF Pro, Segoe UI, and system UI fallbacks. The hierarchy favors large hero typography, restrained card headings, and readable paragraph line-height.

## Spacing

- Page gutter: 16px mobile, 24px tablet, 32px desktop.
- Section rhythm: 80px vertical spacing on major sections.
- Cards: 24px to 32px internal padding.
- Radius: 8px for cards and product surfaces.

## Motion

Motion is intentionally quiet:

- Scroll reveal for major sections.
- Small hover lift on project and repository cards.
- Magnetic button feel through lightweight CSS hover/tap states.
- No neon, game-like, or distracting animation patterns.

## Iconography

Lucide icons are used for navigation, calls to action, metadata, project categories, and contact methods.

## Wireframe

```text
Sticky nav
Hero: personal statement + CTAs | editorial abstract visual
About: engineering story + proof chips
Projects: large case-study cards
Skills: grouped stack cards
Experience: production-judgment timeline
Blog: engineering notes
Contact: role availability + contact details
```
